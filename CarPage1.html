Mercedes-Benz c63s AMG Edition 1

The new Mercedes-AMG C 63 Coupé and C 63 S Coupé are also available as 'Edition 1' special models from market launch. The extraordinary combination of exclusive design and equipment characteristics is inspired by the visual appearance of future Mercedes-AMG racing coupés which will compete in the popular DTM series in 2016. Two exterior designs address different customer wishes. The first variant, available exclusively for the S-Model, is particularly striking: the colour scheme in designo magno selenite grey matt paint with yellow film coating borrows the look of the IAA launch version of the 2016 C 63 DTM and thus highlights its proximity to the world of motorsport. Yellow sports stripes adorn the engine bonnet, roof, boot lid as well as the side lines above the rocker panels.
